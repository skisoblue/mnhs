MNHS bags Best Implementing School Medium Category-Secondary Level

Mabog National High School bags Best Implementing School Medium Category- Secondary Level last June 20, 2022 at the Iloilo Convention Center, Iloilo City, Philippines. The award was received by the school head Leven Mart Lacuna, it serves as a recognition for its exemplary contribution to improve the quality of basic education by highlighting partnership engagements and initiatives to support the Basic Education Learning Continuity Plan through the 2021 Brigada Eskwela Program.
